Our Story

Passionate About Your Comfort

Founded in 2005, HotTub Haven started with a simple mission: make premium hydrotherapy accessible to every home. What began as a small family showroom in California has grown into one of America's most trusted online spa retailers.

Our team of spa engineers and designers work tirelessly to curate a collection that blends cutting-edge technology with timeless elegance. Every hot tub we sell meets our rigorous quality standards.
